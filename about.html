Our Mission

Ford Foundation Grants exists to make critical support accessible to the people who need it most. We focus on individuals—students, caregivers, emerging leaders, and community advocates—who are ready to move forward but face financial barriers.

From clear eligibility criteria to dedicated agent guidance, we keep the process simple, fair, and fast. Our purpose is to empower progress—not with promises, but with practical help.

What We Do

  • Provide targeted grants for qualified individuals
  • Match applicants with an assigned support agent
  • Offer guidance from application to approval
  • Measure outcomes and improve continuously

Our Story

The Ford Foundation Grant program was created with one vision in mind: to empower individuals and communities through financial assistance. Since our inception, we have helped millions of applicants gain access to life-changing support, ensuring that no qualified person is left behind.

2010

Launched our first round of grants to support underprivileged individuals.

2015

Expanded nationwide, with over 500 active field agents.

2020

Surpassed 2 million approved grants with improved online application systems.

Today

Continuing our mission to provide financial assistance and opportunities for a brighter future.
